01

Does Bluecrew actually hire without an interview?

Yes. Bluecrew's model is built around replacing the traditional staffing-agency process. You apply through the app, complete their onboarding steps, and pass a background check. There is no phone screen, no interview panel, and no resume submission. Once approved, you browse available shifts and claim them directly.

02

Is Wag! worth it as income in 2026?

It depends heavily on your area. In denser urban or suburban markets, Wag! caregivers can fill several bookings a week without much marketing. It's most effective as supplemental income alongside another job — the flexible hours make it easy to fit around a fixed schedule. Apply through my caregiver link and you also pick up a $10 bonus after your first completed service.

03

How does getting paid to donate plasma work?

Plasma donation is a screened medical process. Your first visit at BioLife or Octapharma takes longer because of a health screening and paperwork (bring a valid ID, proof of address, and your SSN). After that, return donations are quicker. New-donor bonuses are paid out over your first several visits, and ongoing pay is loaded to a prepaid card. Exact amounts and offers are set by each center and vary by location.

04

Are the Coinbase, Kraken, and Robinhood bonuses really free?

The signup rewards are real, but each has conditions — usually verifying your identity, linking a bank, and in some cases completing a qualifying trade or holding period. Nothing here is financial advice. Crypto and stocks carry risk and you can lose money; only sign up if you understand what you're getting into, and verify current terms in each app.

05

Can I use both Uber and Lyft new-rider offers?

Yes. They're separate apps and separate accounts, so there's no conflict in claiming both. The Lyft offer is invite-code based (enter KEATON38588 in the app); Uber's applies through the link.

Make sure you used a brand-new account and completed all the steps listed for that service. If everything checks out and it's still not showing, contact the platform's in-app support — they can look up the referral attribution on their end. Most issues are resolved within 24–72 hours.
